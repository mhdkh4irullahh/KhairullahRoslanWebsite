My Education

The Journey of My Education

I attended primary school at SK Bukit Selambau. After that I went to school at SMK Bukit Selambau from form one to form 5. At SMK Bukit Selambau I was appointed prefect when I was in form 5. The picture displayed was when I attended the year -end banquet to receive the award as school prefect.

After graduating from high school, I participated in a pre-diploma program at UiTM Kedah. This program is known as MDAB. This pre -diploma program is implemented for 6 months, which is one semester. In this program I was able to learn how to communicate well and get to know many people. This MDAB program had a very positive impact on me when I furthered my studies to Diploma.

Now I am at the Diploma level. I continued my studies at the diploma level in the Information Management course at UiTM Merbok Kedah. In this course I learned a lot about libraries and information management. It is now in the final semester which is semester 5 and will be finishing my studies soon.
